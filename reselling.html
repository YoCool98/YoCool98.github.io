Become a Cool Reseller

Thank you for your interest in reselling Cool! We're excited to explore the opportunity to partner with you. To ensure a smooth process, we’d like to learn a bit more about your shop and its operations.

To get started, please contact ImCytox on Discord with the following information:

  • Your Shop/Business Name
  • How long you've been reselling
  • Reselling Platform: Which platforms do you use for reselling? Please provide a link to your website if available.
  • Payment Methods: What payment methods do you accept from your customers?

Once you’re ready to move forward, you’ll need to register an account on our dashboard. This dashboard will provide you with essential tools and information, including tracking the status and expiration of your keys, and the ability to revoke them if necessary. Additionally, your store will be featured on our purchase page, and you will be recognized as a verified Cool reseller.

Please note: Payments for reselling are exclusively accepted in cryptocurrency. The terms for fronted keys are negotiable.

Product Overview: For a comprehensive overview of all our products, including detailed descriptions, features, and updates, please visit our website.

For any questions or further discussions, please contact ImCytox on Discord.

We look forward to hearing from you and discussing this exciting opportunity further!
